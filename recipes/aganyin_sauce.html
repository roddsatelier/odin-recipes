Ingredients

  • Cup of dried crushed chilies/peppers (Sanbo gbigbe) soaked in warm water for about 3 hours
  • dried bell pepper (Tatase gbigbe)
  • regular red bell pepper (Tatase)
  • Onions
  • Seasoning cube
  • Salt to taste
  • Cups palm oil
  • Beans/black-eyed peas
